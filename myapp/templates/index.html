About

This website provides a detailed analysis on select biotech companies
from an investment and value perspective.

Focusing on the cell/gene therapy, immunotherapy, and gene editing space

These are a few of the highlights of what my analyses contain:

  • Detailed overview on the company's history, stock performance, and major events
  • Drug portfolio, development stages, and target markets and risks
  • A technology and business overview along with their associated risks
  • Financial statement analysis and final verdict on investment value
